How to Use CityBikes Locator

Welcome to CityBikes Locator! This page provides detailed instructions on how to use our platform to find bike stations in cities around the world.

  1. Search for Bike Locations: Click on the "Find Stations" button on the dashboard to view bike stations near your current location.
  2. Customize Search: Use the "Find Cities" button on the dashboard to all cities with bike locations.
  3. Read Instructions: Visit the "Read Instructions" section on the dashboard to learn more about how to use CityBikes Locator effectively.

Tips for Effective Usage

  • Enable location services on your device for accurate results based on your current location.
  • Check bike availability and rental prices before heading to a bike station to ensure a smooth experience.
  • Bookmark your favorite bike stations for quick access during future visits to the same city.
