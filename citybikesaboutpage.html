Introduction

Welcome to the CityBikes! On this page, you will be able to search for your closest bike station in order to ride around! No matter where you are, or where you are going, you will be able to bike around the area.

Mission Statement

Our mission is getting easy transportation for people when they enter or travel to a new city. When traveling to a new city, you may not be comfortable with the language or culture, so using a bike will be the easiest, most seamless way of transportation.

Community Engagement

We value community feedback and collaboration. Help other tourists out by posting the latest rental prices of local bikes. If anything about the website should be changed, we're always listening for feedback!
